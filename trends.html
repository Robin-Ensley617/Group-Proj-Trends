What is a Trend?

From the great mass of content that is published on many social media forms daily, what actually makes something a trend? A social trend can be noted as a sudden uptake of views, hashtags, and reposts on a particular topic. These trends are rapid in their growth to viewers front pagesand can vary from dances, comedy, gossip, challenges, and more


Current Trends

One Chip Challenge

The One Chip Challenge is a popular trend by thePaquibrand. A challenger consumes the iconic Paqui chip containing powders of some extremly spicy peppers such as the Carolina Reaper and Scorpion Pepper. The Scoville scale is a chart ranking the worlds hottest peppers from its least to hottest peppers. Based on some of the chips ingredients, it contains Carolina Reaper Powder which ranks 2.2 million SHU as one of the worlds hottest peppers! It also contains Scorpion Pepper powder which ranks as second hottest on the scale. The challenger must withstand the heat of the chip for as long as possible or accept defeat!


Past Trends

Cinnamon Challenge

The Cinnamon Challenge became a hit around 2017 as many attempted to beat others records. This challenge tests how long users can last without water after swallowing a spoonfull of cinnamon powder. Many fail as they are immediately engulfed in clouds of cinnamon smoke! Although comical, this challenge is extremly dangerous causing irritation to the throat and inflammation to the lungs. This great irritation to the lungs from the cinnamon can lead to pneumonia, a collapsed lung, and respiratory damage.


The Dangers

With millions of likes, shares and views, social media trends can be a source of entertainment and newfound connections with others. In addition, these trends are great ways to promote and grow an online audience or even advertise buisnesses and products. Yet some social media trends pose great risks that are sadly replicated by many youth on these online platforms. Throughout the years, many that have participated in some dangerous trends experience extensive injuries, hospitilization, and even death. One online trend called the Blackout Challenge shows users holding their breath until they passout from a lack of oxygen. This extended lack of oxygen can lead to brain damage or even death.
